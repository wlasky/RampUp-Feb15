A blog about looking for apartments in Manhattan

Looking for apartments in Manhattan can be difficult. I have created this blog to help you simplify your search by providing essential information.

What this blog will cover

This blog will cover the different types of rental apartments. These apartments include:
  • co-ops
  • management run rentals
  • different neighborhoods
  • different fee structures
