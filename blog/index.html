What is a management run rental?

A management run rental is your typical rental. It is an apartment building managed by a rental company that ordinarily owns many different properties.

What are these like in general

Management run rentals run the gamut of apartment styles, from walk ups to luxury highrises. Generally speaking there is a business plan behind them designed to maximize profits. This means that often times they aren't a nice as the property of a single individual. On the other hand, they can ocassionally be better maintained. They are usually as expensive as possible.

Who are these for

  • Someone who doesn't mind spending market rates
  • Someone who doesn't want to deal with a private owner
  • Someone who wants to avoid paying a broker fee by going direct to a management.
